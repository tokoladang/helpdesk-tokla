## **Cara pembayaran VA Bank Jateng melalui CMS Pemda**

1. Buka URL CMS Bank Jateng di https://gibs.bankjateng.co.id/
2. Login menggunakan akun CMS yang sudah terdaftar

![Login CMS](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEj5rs2pRaGBwhXttt0EU0BY_HvMOMPMqXFtioaBKtF05pFf2oIjCnRqi8Okg6djaJqbjSSFZzlciv948D5seAj0FizLnU3CmmEYadujdHyzCgHGP-7Q6Gc4hj-IOtGBbAoLWoDmvNYp1hhsIDKdwAFSXGFvm4iepl6eOpBWPvSfTNI0lCAeM-3Sf_kPqvA/s16000-rw/1.png)

3. Setelah berhasil login, pilih menu **Transfer** pada dashboard

![Menu Transfer](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgT-ovw464KrUdPFrPOLAeZk63pGyFQ-vbr5jflNTS_w6GaxRVVR9-GUE2q7ML56Wwxio-yaUlSKNEPr-e19rtUSRBWRatTaI9qrOUtr29zp37NR2RPbpVB89PHpoOs392gjCtR5Dgn6rYRxr7ltFr79W82yortZ5R3VPUQmWOL88IF9aecKVLezjosbHA/s16000-rw/2.png)

4. Pilih submenu **Transfer Rekening Lain**

![Transfer Rekening Lain](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhN3jTbQzAb4m3azbmZr2NTf3dYBbwCWfsiVFnRTzLbp1QJsrwjj72LSJN5zGxmiLdQjUkpx2rV4cdJwvoDXb_CY-03CcCRw2F3ufMiZCFNvbKTWEPf2bfC1flBb27TmVW2YTjDJoh2RcPb9YCXTaTAdu2Evg6aI-Rb5ayaWGat_gFKO7DCl-3YX5fnuzg/s16000-rw/3.png)

5. Lengkapi form transfer:
    - **Dari Rekening**: Pilih rekening sumber
    - **Ke Rekening**: Masukkan nomor Virtual Account (VA) Toko Ladang
    - **Nominal**: Masukkan jumlah pembayaran
    - **Keterangan**: Isi keterangan jika diperlukan

![Form Transfer](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjmcoc_ACU2cVT-JK67ULkj7MzUNkRysTexP3TO8gp6R6acg4d4zMZCKtB7vuQ9PlOpX-jph-rDA6igRFTkTN02WOAhn5ziwjCVgw3QENmaiBnbZ50HI4RDEm8H7wEYuwafQqyJ5FPsPOS1MNwvXbF2WoWp96QmZM-3fOz9rh8jV-zAZufwGtIr4mjy6WU/s16000-rw/4.png)

6. Klik **Proses** untuk melanjutkan

![Proses Transfer](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgMikZARvdxv0UlY01-GSol4raEsw29_PsXeLnQClT9lp4T7GvTtjZ7jXCwR8EDPVgjrNxvffliXQj4n0Losve2V6C4UQUdyRfB6lTKoSAeI_51jlwtW-3h1RON2f63w9SmC5Col7SpYoT_o1NuZWI8WCl0079VWxfdTX8MhfajtKu63GtI0VqIbDLHhAU/s16000-rw/5.png)

7. Masukkan **PIN / password transaksi** untuk validasi

![Validasi PIN](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhCUqj-iJjlNc6W_D9ReAGYlvn6-CefsAgb2VcLHRDxJbU_dLKep0csJWrDjy3PnXBfcsoZdOQQbgtQaxeUVDC3sPPACjuE54MNb6lDjYLHUx5WgsLUViPSHy8S4d54PuK3QsROGoHixlesebBD367FKPHfNsYCxDyyaMA15tUQmKuo1yeB7hRMy6hwrUs/s16000-rw/6.png)

8. Klik **Submit** / **Kirim** untuk memproses transaksi

![Submit](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhudPw_cVI-bvKBaXogO-3E4XHOKu6GdnCwi_03JYYHUnzfFh6K0HgPtcmMqDSL16Iz9ki8UADOl0zgBZqDvJyA62EtoufCgxkXExMelL_3m2r400Kn3fedFkX02HN9o2-Oq6gTIVKibLgp4eOSJPVy_Oc3FKBx-yEcnOukosAyy7O61cZjhE9HVocN2Gg/s16000-rw/7.png)

9. Selanjutnya, login menggunakan **User Approver** untuk menyetujui transaksi

![Login Approver](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjXcQ303VyxbS_64kVDV7CKQEIw-ns-1cj7qAwqPQGBTPKx1EqFHRdOaGM4v-3gS4-1l1c3Qga-lME-pnrvWlov_WfRQloTjlChR1Xw-cHSJ_NZQfbxr5lynpIZS-Zf4PTBE79LkVjydNnzL-f_LPM4PXFI832-GkALu3ctD-je7LFWEU5xkYrOUd1uX1k/s16000-rw/8.png)

10. Pilih menu **Tugas Penyetuju** > **Transfer**

![Tugas Penyetuju](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjnkYREuGa4N3uaQNu_3L6KIjHLtaXU2DQLFyLljlSxAnJRnTrqAVFPK8PVgov0uyu3TiW2zpOCrTxLoR_bhXlJSGKcp2fMsu0xFIxMluDFcl7ENSbJN9iCaFckXLgvxJ1aUB2xfGVQO2WE07ZcIAV7m9vDrWFh1P5EjcA7hlY8Ku3M1mAaqVcCpcjnY94/s16000-rw/9.png)

11. Klik **Setuju** untuk mengotorisasi transaksi

![Setujui Transaksi](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEgrXBzrN4Gbc0j0RVqs5pU0K_cyS4ZRMMZiyBfoQ207Q0Nn_wtYJ0gg4CDj-ky-IEac7OtsWRMJw8BazcrHV50r_IFrR-pNSRcjrNIGMRfZ8TRcTLqUA4h4IJrU-6179Yb9IVmB1AvnyzEF2oJNJGSalU33JfKioH49yWI7yQe3O0Udkwf3ZEU_KEIsgqE/s16000-rw/10.png)

12. Transaksi berhasil diproses. Simpan bukti transaksi untuk dokumentasi

![Transaksi Berhasil](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEhO1s3w2jNgaU5rSawfgwsVZrbvjZrICdZl1yXHb_04zlLoXkSsMI5vmaf2h3nFBxPZ1fhglHELL1Ve3AXShgc5hj32cJh5gVrL-DC6s4EQZN7uWGurnAGWb0Fqy-M4hRcIWEB8qs613NQbieh5OYJLOpDXLFuNRyB0Oz0uhcwWGEADdZGe6PrulU6WbM0/s16000-rw/11.png)
